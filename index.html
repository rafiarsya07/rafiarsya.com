Hi there! I'm Rafi

I am currently studying Bachelor of Computer Science (Software Engineering) at the University of Malaya, Malaysia. I focus on software engineering and full-stack development, and I enjoy coding because I like building systems that are useful and solve real problems. My interest in web development started back in school, which became the beginning of my journey in programming.

I previously worked as the Head of Website Division, Data and Information System Bureau at PPI Malaysia, where I led the team responsible for developing and maintaining the official PPI Malaysia website. My responsibility was to ensure the website runs well, stays updated, and remains easy to use for Indonesian students in Malaysia.

I also served as the Head of Department for the Art Exhibition at IDFEST (PPI University of Malaya). In this role, I managed the exhibition from planning to execution, making sure everything was well organized and delivered properly to showcase Indonesian culture to an international audience. In addition, I took part as a volunteer in the Transportation Team (PPI Malaysia 2026), where I worked with other volunteers to support transportation and logistics during the event, focusing on coordination and teamwork to ensure smooth operations.

Outside of coding, I enjoy sharing knowledge and explaining technical topics in a simple and easy-to-understand way. I also like working with others, taking on new challenges, and building things that are meaningful and practical.
